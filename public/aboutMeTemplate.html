About Me
My name is Kevon Boxill and I am a self-taught Software Developer. I started this journey about over a year ago. I have always been an avid follower of the computer arts but would always be to afraid to dive into computer programming.
I had the notion that it would be filled with 1's and 0's, but to my surprise it was totally easy. I am always looking for new knowledge and ways to expand my knowledge.
Althought I am and will always still be on my journey to becoming an internationally recognised Software Developer, looking back on my progress, I feel proud to call myself a software developer.
